Renal Transplant Ultrasound with Doppler – patent artery/vein; RI 0.68–0.74

Indication: POD#1 post kidney transplant; evaluate perfusion.

Technique: Ultrasound with color and spectral Doppler of transplant kidney in right iliac fossa.

Findings:
- Kidney size: 11.2 cm, normal echogenicity, no hydronephrosis.
- Main renal artery and vein patent with normal flow.
- Resistive indices: 0.68–0.74.
- No perinephric collections.

Impression:
Normal post-transplant appearance. Patent vessels with normal resistive indices. No evidence of obstruction or vascular compromise.
