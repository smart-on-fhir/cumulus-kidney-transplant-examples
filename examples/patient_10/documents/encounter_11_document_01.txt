IR Procedure Note – Ultrasound-Guided Renal Allograft Biopsy – 2025-08-29

Indication:
Worsening creatinine, subtherapeutic tacrolimus levels, and presence of new DSAs.

Procedure:
Under ultrasound guidance, three core samples obtained from right iliac fossa renal allograft using 18G biopsy needle. Hemostasis achieved with manual compression. No immediate complications. Patient monitored in recovery for 4 hours.

Findings:
Adequate tissue obtained for pathologic evaluation.

Disposition:
Patient discharged home same day with instructions for rest, monitoring for hematuria, pain, or fever. Await pathology.
