Immunosuppression Initiation & Dosing Plan – Tacrolimus/MMF/Prednisone
Encounter: 2 | Document: 2 | Date: 2017-10-13
Induction
- Basiliximab 20 mg IV day 0 and planned day 4.
- Methylprednisolone 500 mg IV intra-op.

Maintenance (initial)
- Tacrolimus 2 mg PO BID; target trough 8–10 ng/mL for first 3 months; first level tomorrow AM prior to dose.
- Mycophenolate mofetil 1,000 mg PO BID.
- Prednisone taper: 60 mg daily → gradual taper to 5 mg daily by ~3 months.

Infection Prophylaxis
- Valganciclovir 450 mg PO daily x3 months (CMV D+/R+; adjust per eGFR).
- TMP-SMX SS PO daily x6 months (PCP).
- Fluconazole 100 mg PO weekly x3 months (if endemic risk).

Monitoring
- Tacrolimus trough daily while inpatient; then 2–3x/week outpatient initially.
- CBC, CMP, Mg, Phos at each draw; watch for leukopenia and nephrotoxicity.
