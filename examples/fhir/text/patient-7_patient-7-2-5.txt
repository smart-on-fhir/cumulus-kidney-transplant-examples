Immunosuppression Plan – Tacrolimus/MMF/Prednisone with valganciclovir and SMX-TMP prophylaxis

Initial Immunosuppression Regimen:
- Tacrolimus 2 mg PO BID (target trough 8–10 ng/mL)
- Mycophenolate mofetil 1 g PO BID
- Prednisone 20 mg PO daily

Induction Therapy:
- Basiliximab POD#0 and POD#4

Prophylaxis:
- Valganciclovir 450 mg daily x 6 months (CMV donor+/recipient+)
- TMP-SMX SS daily x 12 months (PJP prophylaxis)
- Nystatin swish and swallow while inpatient

Monitoring:
- Daily tacrolimus trough until stable
- Weekly CMV PCR x 3 months
- Monthly BK virus PCR x 6 months
- DSA monitoring at baseline, 1 month, 3 months
